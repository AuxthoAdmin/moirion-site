FAQ

Common questions

Do I need an account?

No. You can start using Moirion locally without creating an account.

Does my data leave my computer?

Not by default. Your notes stay on your machine unless you choose to export, upload, or connect something.

Do I need a local model before I start?

No. You can begin by writing and keeping notes first. Add a local model later if you want deeper answers.

What is Moirion for?

Moirion is for notes, records, and documents you want to keep and return to later.
