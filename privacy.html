Privacy

A local system should stay legible about privacy.

Moirion is designed around local-first use. This page explains the public-facing baseline without pretending every workflow is identical.

Local by default

Moirion is built so your notes, records, and documents can stay on your machine by default.

Explicit actions matter

If you export, upload, connect an external service, or choose a remote workflow, that action is explicit. The product should not make data movement feel invisible.

Optional local models

You do not need to install a local model before you begin. If you add one later, the experience remains local-first.

Support

For product questions or data-related requests, contact hello@auxtho.com.
